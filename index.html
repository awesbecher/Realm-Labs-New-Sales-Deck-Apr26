Deep Neural Inspection

See what the model is thinking. Not just what it says.

Legacy AI safety tools scan inputs and outputs. Realm reads the model's internal state while it thinks. The same approach Anthropic, Google DeepMind, and OpenAI use internally to make frontier models safer, turned into a production runtime platform.

01

The old way: pattern matching.

Legacy filters scan prompts and outputs for keywords. Rephrase the prompt and the filter misses it. A confident hallucination slips right through. I/O inspection is a metal detector: it catches the obvious, not the invisible.

02

The breakthrough: mechanistic interpretability.

A new field of AI research reverse-engineers what a neural network is actually computing, step by step. Frontier labs use it internally to understand and steer their own models. Realm Labs is the first to turn it into an enterprise runtime product.

03

The result: Deep Neural Inspection.

Realm taps a live model's internal activations, classifies them against thousands of behaviors in real time, and enforces policy in under 50 milliseconds. Deterministic, explainable, reproducible. No LLM-as-judge. No regex. No guesswork.
