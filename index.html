The Invisible Failures

The 8 ways your AI silently fails.

01 The Confidence Trap
Confidently wrong.
Wrong answer, full conviction. No hedging, no uncertainty markers. Sounds authoritative, which is why nobody catches it.
02 The Silent Mismatch
Answered the wrong question.
Valid response, wrong target. The user asked A, the model answered B. User often can't articulate what's off.
03 The Drift
Wandered off task.
Early turns on topic. By turn five the conversation has drifted from the original goal. No single turn is wrong, the trajectory is.
04 The Death Spiral
Loops, never resolves.
Same issue, different words. The model rewords its non-answer until the user retries and gets the same non-answer again.
05 The Walkaway
User gave up.
No complaint. No thumbs-down. No ticket. The user just stopped. Your tools see nothing. Churn sees everything.
06 The Contradiction Unravel
Reversed itself across turns.
Turn three says yes. Turn seven says no. Neither turn is flagged alone. The contradiction only exists across the full trace.
07 The Partial Recovery
Almost got there.
Most of the answer is right, one critical detail is wrong. Good enough to accept, dangerous enough to act on.
08 The Mystery Failure
Failed and we can't say why.
Something went wrong, the output was bad, the logs are silent. Without reasoning-chain visibility, there's nothing to debug.
